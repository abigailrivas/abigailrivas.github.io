Selected projects

Campus Virtual

A completely redesign of the virtual campus of my uni.

Work I did: user research, wireframes, ux writing, prototyping, visual design.

The problem

Lack of user-friendly navigation and communication, annoying popups, outdated design, bad notification system. As a result, students struggle to find easily and fast the information they need.

The solution

1. Home redesign: Easy access to courses, easy navigation and provides clear information.

2. A notification icon: Users will know when a video or a file was updated, or if there are new messages and announcements.

3. A section for news: No more popups!

4. Calendar redesign: Indicates if you have an exam or another assigments.

5. Recently see section: It allows you an easy access to the last content you saw.

6. Friendly message: A motivational or kind message to students to have a more enjoyable experience.
